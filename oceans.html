Serene Beaches

Clean and serene beaches give a person some peace. Long walks on those serene beaches with the sound of waves and collecting seashells sound almost perfect. Listening to the waves and getting a sunbath sound like some ideal peaceful time. An ideal beach vacation might differ from person to person. Keeping the beaches clean is also our responsibility and not just of the cleaners.

  1. Radhanagar Beach,Andaman& Nicobar Island

    This is the second-longest beach in Asia. It is famous for its unique sand texture. One can’t afford to miss the sunset from this beach. The scenery is breathtaking as well as the orange pink purple sky is something which you cannot forget once you see it.

    Best Time To Visit: October to May.

  2. Marari Beach, Kerala

    Alleppey (Alappuzha) is famous for its backwaters and houseboat stays. This beach isn’t yet discovered by many people and is calm, peaceful and quiet. It’s the best place to visit if you wish to spend some quality time with yourself. Find your inner peace. It is bordered by a small fishing village named Mararikulam.

    Best Time To Visit: December to March.

  3. Talsari Beach,Odisha

    Talasari is one of the most tranquil and unspoiled beaches of Odisha. The name Talasari is literally means (row of Palm leaves in Odia). The waters of Bay of Bengal are very calm which means it’s one of the safest beaches that one can come across.The rows of the Palm trees adds the beauty of the beach, the meandering Subarnarekha river accentuates the visual appeal of the beach by a few notches, the sand dunes and little red crabs lends character to the beach, the fishing hamlets and the nearby mangroves of Bichitrapur gives the location a purpose

    Best Time to Visit:Octoberto December,and Febuary

  4. Baga Beach,Goa

    One of the most happening beaches in Goa, Baga Beach is where you will find water sports, fine dining restaurants, bars, and clubs. Situated in North Goa, Baga Beach is bordered by Calangute and Anjuna Beaches. Watersports are the major attraction of Baga Beach. Parasailing, wakeboarding, windsurfing, kite surfing, jet skiing are some of the top watersports activities to enjoy in Baga Beach. One can also go for dolphin spotting tours and island trips from the beach. Baga also boasts one of the best nightlife scenes in Goa, the famous Tito’s Lane has some of the best bars and clubs. One unique attraction at Baga Beach is an indoor snow park that has a play area, sledding area, and ice bar. For shopaholics, there is a Tibetan Market on the Baga Road.

    Best Time to Visit:March to October
